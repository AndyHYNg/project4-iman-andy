About

Revelio is a social network for the wizarding world. The name is inspired by the Homenum Revelio charm, which reveals the presence of humans in the vicinity.

How do I use Revelio?

It's easy! Just type in a character name to look up anyone ("Harry", "Remus", "Tom Riddle") or type in other references to get a list of matching individuals ("Gryffindor", "Death eater", "ghost").
